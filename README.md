Europe Sales Performance Dashboard

The Europe Sales Dashboard provides a clear view of sales performance across regions, product categories, and time. It helps users quickly understand how the business is performing and where improvements can be made.

Solutions Provided
Identifies top-performing products to support better sales strategies
Highlights underperforming categories that may need attention
Reveals sales trends over time for planning and forecasting
Breaks down performance by region to guide market focus
Enables quick decision-making through interactive filters and visuals

Purpose
This dashboard turns raw sales data into simple, actionable insights, making it easier for stakeholders to monitor performance and make informed business decisions.
